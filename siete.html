About Me

The Youngest Legend | Retro Soul. Racer Heart. Name’s Rouie Solamo, but in the TRP , they call me “Siete” a nod to my custom-built café racer, clean and classic, just like the number 7 in Latin. I’m the youngest rider in the crew, but don’t mistake youth for weakness I ride with purpose, style, and heart. Before I hit the throttle, I played the field a football player by blood, but the streets and steel found me. Now, I channel that same passion into every curve and chrome of my build. Vintage vibes. Retro roots. Full respect. That’s what I stand for. My café racer? One of the cleanest in the game. They call me the “Youngest Legend” in Tranquil Reapers RC not just for the ride, but for the soul I bring to it. Style is loud. Loyalty is louder. TRP for life.
